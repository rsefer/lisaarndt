Ever since I was a child growing up in northeastern Ohio, I have been fascinated with baking, Especially watching my mother, and her mother, making sugar cookies for the holidays. I carried on this cookie tradition as a young adult and was often told, "You should sell these!" So, I have been doing just that since 2008.

Each and everyone of my creations are lovingly baked with the freshest and natural ingredients, They are delicious on the inside and equally enticing on the outside. Each treat is handmade and meticulously decorated to celebrate your event. My sweet decorated offerings can be the focal point of your event, or the perfect personalized gift for someone special.

Whether it be weddings, parties, business gatherings, or a simple thank you gift, my treats will surely put a smile on everyone's face. My cookies will delight your tastebuds and melt in your mouth. I've always had a love for sweets and a passion for my cookies and cupcakes to be enjoyed by all. Contact me to bake you a batch of my sensational sweets for your next special occasion and I will hand deliver these handmade goodies!
